Upgrade Recommendations:

Converting a live system using the upgrade system isn`t recommended. Ideally its best to attempt the upgrade on a local server to make sure everything goes ok. I recommend duplicating your existing setup on localhost and upgrading that one to see what happens. After you can re-import your database files and re-upload the system. If something goes wrong with a live site you`ll have to take it down.

Whichever way you choose, uprading is entirely at your own risk. Always backup your system before you begin.
